Tyler Ochart a versatile freelance cinematographer, filmmaker, and editor. Born in Los Angeles, Ca some of Tylers most memorable childhood moments include going to the movies, and seeing iconic films such as Batman Returns & Jurassic Park just to name a few. Seeing these iconic films sparked a passion for filmmaking for many years to come. The exact moment Tyler was able to grasp a camera in his hands he began to film small and tell stories with friends and family creating a number of different projects such as small skits and documentaries. As an adult Tyler was stuck at a career plateau working at a customer service job for the past ten years Tyler realized it was time for a change. Tyler discovered his love for telling stories through a lens was more then just any old hobby he decided to pursue his passion professionally, and in 2019 enrolled in Full Sail university to obtain his bachelors of science degree and pursue a career in cinematogrsaphy. Fast forward to 2022 Tyler Ochart conquered his dream graduating from Full Sail university as Valedictorian & receiving his Bachelors degree in Cinematography.
